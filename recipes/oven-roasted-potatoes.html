Oven Roasted Potatoes

Description

A great roasted potatoe sid dish made with olive oil and herbs.

Ingredients

  • ⅛ cup of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced in garlic
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 245 Â°C
  2. Combine oil, garlic, basil, marjoram, dill, thyme, oregano, parsley, pepper flakes and salt in a large bowl; stir in potatoes until evenly coated. Places potatoes in a single layer on a roasting pan or baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven, turning occasionally to brown on all sides, until tender, about 20 to 30 minutes.

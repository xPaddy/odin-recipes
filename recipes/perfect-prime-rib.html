Perfect Prime Rib

Description

Recipe for the perfect prime rib. Have fun and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) prime rib roast
  • ½ unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon herbs de Provence
  • kosher salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place prime rib roast on a plate and bring to room temperature, 2 to 4 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 260 degrees C.
  3. Combine butter, pepper, and herbes de Provence in a bowl; mix it until well blended. Spread butter mixture evently over entire roast. Season roast generously with kosher salt.
  4. Roast the 4-pound roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. (If your roast is larger or smaller than 4 pounds, multiply the exact weight time 5 minutes.)
  5. Turn the oven off and, leaving the roast in the oven with the door closed, let the roast sit in the oven for 2 hours.
  6. Remove the roast from the oven, slice, and serve.

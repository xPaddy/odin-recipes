Spinach Strawnerry Salad

Description

A tasty salad with fresh spinach and strawberries.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • strawberries
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Poppy Seeds
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Toss together spinach and strawberries in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl. Pour over the soinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
